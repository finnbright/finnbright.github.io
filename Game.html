Game Dev

Human Operations Worldwide

Human Operations Worldwide, or HOW, is a video game I have worked on for about a year and a half and plan to release soon. It is single player horror game where you have to escape a large facility formerly used for medical prosthetics. You will have to use keys to search through the many rooms found in the game while also avoiding the monsters that lurk throughout the building. This has been one of my largest accomplishments and I believe it shows my skills of artistic design, modeling, game design, and coding.

Todd's Ritual

Todd's Ritual is another video game I have been working on and have many plans for. It is a horror game set in your characters bedroom where you have to preform tasks that the monster orders you to do. There are various interactions you can complete from picking up objects to finding hidden doors.
